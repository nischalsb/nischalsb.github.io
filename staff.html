Our Team

Educate Nepal Initiative (EduN) is an organization of young minds enriched with dedication and determination to secure a change, a positive change for the future of Nepal in the sector of Education. Inaugurated in 2073/1/18, EduN is a result of dynamic youths from across the country which in itself is an indicator on how scenarios and circumstances of our societies are taken into account to design the welfare programs that it aims to do.

Asutosh Karki Executive Member

I am a energetic youth from Nepal and with a continuous effort in conducting pragmatic research, co-creating knowledge with people to improve the situation as my contribution to creating a better world. My critical thinking, communication skills, and social research background are the driving notion to continuously make a difference in the community. To this day, I had taken the initiative to lead crowdsourcing initiative in OpenStreetMap(OSM) for Nepal called LightsON, where public utilities, Street Lights, are mapped. I have believed public utilities are people's property and public themselves must become wary of this facility provided by the state and safeguard its utility. As part of this crowdsourcing leadership, I encouraged individuals from five colleges, two social organizations to participate in this crowdsourced activity by going out in the streets and collect data on street lights utilizing a data collection platform. This is my step and commitment to creating a better tomorrow.
