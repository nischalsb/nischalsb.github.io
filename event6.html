Gossip stories - I


Venue: Cocina Mitho Cha
Time: 12:00 pm – 3:00 pm
Date: 10th November 2017


Our Guests: Mr. Sudeep Acharya

S.N. Particulars Amount
1. Venue Cost 20,000/-
2. Banners and Flex 15,000/-
3. Promotions (Facebook and other online promotions) 5,000/-
4. Snacks and drinks (After the program ends) 10,000/-
5. Honors 5,000/-
6. Miscellaneous 15,000/-
Total 70,000/-


Gossip stories - An informal setting with formal stories, informal stories with formal values was what Gossip Stories was all about. The program was all set on Cocina Mitho Chha, Lazimpat with audiences ranging in age. The audiences were largely from Kathmandu Valley but we also had guests from various parts of the country. The program started as it was set. We had Mr Sudip Acharya recalling his times and sharing his memories and moments, experiences, ups and downs. His journey on DishHome was something that young and aspiring could look up to. ‘Hurdles are nothing more but mere stepping stones to success’.

‘A problem is a problem if you think so if you take it as an opportunity to solve, no one can stop you,’ are few of the notable mentions by the speaker to motivate the youths. The success stories of the successful Sudip Acharya was brought down to the table and shared among the audiences. The aim of the program was achieved as inspiration was what most of the audiences claimed to achieve. EDUN, believing in stories make stories were what led to making this program, and the stories were what made this program a huge success.

We could not have been able to complete the program without the support of our valuable sponsors. The institutions and the individuals who put forward their hand for the help of the needy ones are: Our sponsors
● Cocina Mitho Cha
● Pawan Dhital
● Mukti Ram Pandey
● Santosh Ghimire

As a whole, the program was a grand success, where we were able to communicate with the people belonging to various backgrounds and share them with inspiring stories to inspire them to become the youths they aspire to be.
