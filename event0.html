Skill Development Program at Achham

Venue: Different schools of Achham, Nepal.
Date: 16th January 2022

Our objectives


● To help build English Reading and speaking skills along with leadership skills.
● To develop problem-solving skills and collaboration.
● To students become independent thinkers through MUN and encourages them to plan for their future.
● To better communication, time management and negotiation skills
● To promote creativity through art and debate.
● To help them gain scientific knowledge through experiments

In the 21st Century, skills are more important than degree. This program was conducted with the aim of harnessing the skills of students in government schools of Achham who are deprived of resource. Through this program, we taught those students about English Speaking and Writing by forming a Reading Club, Debate, Computer Basics like typing, MS Word, MS Powerpoint, Google Chrome, digital education(MeroSiksha, Khan Academy, Youtube). With the support of professional, we also gave lessons about calligraphy. We also taught students Science by doing science experiments and invention in association with Antarikshya Pratisthan.
