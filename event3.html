'नेपालमा भ्रष्टाचार नै छैन?' - Youth Summit 2018


Venue: Rastriya Sabha Griha, Pradarshani Marg, Kathmandu, Nepal.
Time: 10:00 – 3:30 pm
Date: 1st May 2018

Our Guests: Mr. Ram Karki
Mr. Yogesh Bhattarai
Mr. Min Bahadur Bishwakarma
Mr. Nain Singh Mahar
Mr. Govinda Narayan

Our objectives
● Aware youths about the drawbacks of our nation’s governance.
● Aware youths about the acts of corruption prevailing in our nation.
● Aware the youths about the cause and effects of corruption to the nation.
● Suggest things that can be done by the youths to address this problem..
● Make audience informed about our organization and why?
● Maintain good relation among youths throughout the nation.
● Increase the networking among the youths.

S.N. Particulars Amount
1. Photographer (Cameraman and videographer) 80,000/-
2. Venue Cost 30,000/-
3. Banners and Flex 30,000/-
4. Promotions (Facebook and other online promotions) 25,000/-
5. Snacks and drinks (After the program ends) 2,00,000/-
6. MC cost 30,000/-
7. Sound System 25,000/-
8. Honors 20,000/-
9. Miscellaneous 20,000/-
Total 4,50,000/-

The program started as per the schedule. The program then started with an introduction of the guests of the program by the host. Initially, the audience was told about our organization. Then the guests were asked to provide their valuable words to the mass.

The overall program was a grand success with the involvement of more than ……???? people. We were able to inform the youths on the aimed agenda and make necessary measures applicable. The audience we had were largely from the Kathmandu Valley. Even though we focus our reach on the youths, this program also had guests belonging to the other age-group. The program revolved around the agenda ‘Corruption and it’s existence’. We were able to provide the youth with an insight into the drawbacks, flaws, weaknesses of our nation’s governance. As Corruption was the main basis to making this program into a reality, the youths were informed on the acts of corruption prevailing in our country, the things that lead to this problem and the consequences to the nation.

We could not have been able to complete the program without the support of our valuable sponsors. The institutions and the individuals who put forward their hand for the help of the needy ones are:
● Sparrow power of text
● Khalti online digital partner
● Buddha Air
● Samrat Tours and Travels Pvt. Ltd.

As a whole, the program was a grand success, where we were able to communicate with the youths from different parts of the country and aware them about the underlying opportunities in our nation.
