'नेपालमै अवसर छ' - Youth Summit 2017


Venue: DAV school, Jawalakhel, Lalitpur, Nepal.
Time: 1:00 – 4:00 pm
Date: 28th Oct 2017

Our Guests:
Rabindra Basnet
Bhushan Dahal
DayaHang Rai

Our objectives
● Aware youths about the opportunities hidden in Nepal.
● Aware youths how hard work pays through the stories of the speakers.
● Identify and circulate ideas that can be executed by the youths in different fields such as: media, politics, artist, sports and more.
● Make the audience aware about our organization
● Maintain good relation among youths throughout the nation.
● Increase the networking among the youths.

S.N. Particulars Amount
1. Photographer (Cameraman and videographer) 80,000/-
2. Venue Cost 30,000/-
3. Banners and Flex 30,000/-
4. Promotions (Facebook and other online promotions) 25,000/-
5. Snacks and drinks (After the program ends) 2,00,000/-
6. MC cost 30,000/-

8. Honors 25,000/-
9. Miscellaneous 25,000/-
Total 4,70,000/-


The program started as per the schedule. The program then started with an introduction of the guests of the program by the host. Initially, the audience was told about our organization. Then the guests were asked to provide their valuable words to the mass. Firstly, Mr Rabindra Basnet started his talk by sharing his experience of work during his tenure at the BBC. He told about the difficulties and steps he took to overcome such hurdles. He compared the systems of our nation Nepal and of the foreign developed countries he has been too.

Then, Mr Bhushan Dahal was requested to share his valuable words with the youths. He talked about the difficulties in the media industry and the way to follow if the youths are interested in the field of media. He shared the valuable skills he acquired in his school days in Budhanilkantha School and in other colleges later on which always motivated him and took him to the respectable height he is in today.

After him, the actor of Nepalese film industry Mr Dayahang Rai shared his experience of hard work. He talked about his early lifestyle and the things that really motivated him to be in the place he has reached today. He shared the experience of working in small types of dramas and short movies to many huge and top hit movies in the Nepalese film industry. He shared about his difficulties and the decisions he made which always uplifted his standard of acting and working in the field of the Nepalese film industry.

We could not have been able to complete the program without the support of our valuable sponsors. The institutions and the individuals who put forward their hand for the help of the needy ones are:
● Sparrow power of text
● Khalti online digital partner
● Buddha Air
● Samrat Tours and Travels Pvt. Ltd.

As a whole, the program was a grand success, where we were able to communicate with the youths from different part of the country and aware them about the hidden opportunities in our nation. Through the program, not only the youths were provided with insight but also had the opportunity to listen to the experience of successful people in different fields who achieved everything through their hard work from their days of youth.
