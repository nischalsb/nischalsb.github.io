Our Team

Educate Nepal Initiative (EduN) is an organization of young minds enriched with dedication and determination to secure a change, a positive change for the future of Nepal in the sector of Education. Inaugurated in 2073/1/18, EduN is a result of dynamic youths from across the country which in itself is an indicator on how scenarios and circumstances of our societies are taken into account to design the welfare programs that it aims to do.

Dhirendra Acharya President

Dhirendra Acharya is the President of the Education Nepal Initiative (EduN), a role that reflects his deep commitment to driving positive change in Nepal's education sector. With a strong academic background with MBA at Global College, Dhirendra has equipped himself with the knowledge and skills necessary to lead effectively. His educational journey began at Xavier International School, followed by studies at Caribbean College and Brooklyn College, culminating in a Bachelor’s in Social Work (B.S.W.). Dhirendra's leadership extends beyond the classroom. He has served as a member secretary under the Ministry of Youth and Sports' Youth and Self-Employment Fund. His experience as a social mobilizer with CEPDRA, Nepal, and his active participation in various social and volunteer activities demonstrate his dedication to community development. A team player with strong communication skills, Dhirendra is goal-driven and adept at managing responsibilities. Fluent in English and Nepali, he is committed to using his abilities to make a meaningful impact. His leadership at EduN continues to inspire and drive forward initiatives aimed at improving education and empowering youth across Nepal.

Tilak Dhital Founder, Vice President

Tilak Prasad Dhital is an emerging leader in the fields of education, finance, and entrepreneurship with over six years of diverse experience. He is a co-founder and Financial Head at Digital Learning Nepal - MeroSiksha, where he has played a pivotal role in raising funds, launching the MVP, and overseeing the finance department. A certified ICT Smart Board Trainer, Tilak has also co-founded Budhanilkantha Education Services, managing classrooms and teaching A-level Economics. Tilak's passion for education extends to his role as Vice-President of the Education Nepal Initiative, where he has organized national-level events and led impactful projects in various districts. His commitment to education and youth empowerment has earned him several accolades, including the Global Students Entrepreneurship Award 2023 and recognition as an Emerging Leader in the Nepal Youth Young Entrepreneurship Award 2024. Academically, Tilak is pursuing an MBA at the Global College of Management and holds a Bachelor's in Development Finance from National College. A former School Captain at Budhanilkantha School, he is also fluent in English and Nepali, with skills in Microsoft Office, Photoshop, and basic programming languages. An avid sports enthusiast, Tilak has competed in national-level swimming and track & field events.

Sulav Khanal Executive Member

Sulav Khanal is a visionary leader dedicated to transforming education through digital technology. As the Co-Founder of MeroSiksha, he has played a crucial role in improving educational access and quality, training over 5,000 students and teachers in effective digital learning practices. His efforts focus on integrating traditional and digital education, creating a more interactive and engaging learning experience for all. Sulav’s work has significantly impacted the educational landscape, bridging gaps and ensuring that learning remains relevant and accessible in the digital age. Beyond his work at MeroSiksha, Sulav has made notable contributions through the Educative Nepal Initiative. He led a successful $8,000 fundraising campaign that provided essential resources to a government school in Rimuwa, Gulmi, opening up new opportunities for students. Additionally, he has spearheaded programs to support widows in western Nepal, helping them gain valuable skills to improve their livelihoods. Sulav is also a passionate public speaker, sharing his experiences and insights on leadership and digital education to inspire others to innovate and drive positive change within their communities.
