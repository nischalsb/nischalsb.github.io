Our Incubation Center

Incubation Center

Welcome to the Educate Nepal Initiative Incubation Center, a dedicated space for nurturing innovative ideas and projects aimed at transforming education. Our center is designed to support educators, social entrepreneurs, and change agents who are passionate about improving educational outcomes and creating equitable learning opportunities for all.

Message from the Coordinator


As the Coordinator, I am thrilled to invite you to our Incubation Center. At Educate Nepal Initiative, we believe that every great educational idea deserves the opportunity to grow and make a lasting impact. Our center is more than just a workspace; it's a community where passion meets purpose. We are here to support you every step of the way as you turn your innovative ideas into reality, creating meaningful change in education.

We look forward to working with you, helping you overcome challenges, and celebrating your successes. Together, we can build a brighter future for education.

- Nitanta Singh Bista, Incubation Center Coordinator


Our Mission

Our mission is to empower those with groundbreaking ideas in education by providing the resources, mentorship, and networks they need to develop and scale their initiatives. We are committed to creating a vibrant ecosystem where educational innovation thrives, leading to impactful solutions that address the pressing challenges in education today.

What We Offer

  • Educational Mentorship & Guidance: Connect with experienced educators, innovators, and thought leaders who will provide personalized mentorship to help you refine your ideas and overcome the challenges of launching and growing your educational project.
  • Professional Development Workshops: Participate in workshops and training sessions focused on key areas such as curriculum development, educational technology, inclusive education practices, and sustainable project management.
  • Collaborative Networking: Become part of a community of educators, NGOs, policymakers, and investors who share a commitment to enhancing education. Our networking events and partnerships offer opportunities for collaboration and growth.
  • Innovative Workspace: Access a creative and collaborative co-working space designed to inspire innovation in education. Our facilities provide a supportive environment where ideas can flourish and projects can be developed.
  • Access to Educational Funding: Explore funding opportunities tailored to educational initiatives, including grants, scholarships, and partnerships with organizations dedicated to advancing education.
  • Resource Center: Utilize our extensive resource center, which includes research materials, case studies, lesson plans, and toolkits designed to support your educational project at every stage.

Get Involved

Are you passionate about making a difference in education? Join our incubation program and be part of a community that is dedicated to creating transformative educational experiences. Apply today and take the first step toward making a lasting impact on learners and communities.
